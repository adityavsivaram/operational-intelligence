The Business Problem

Organizations increasingly rely on ERP dashboards and AI-assisted recommendations. While these technologies improve visibility and prediction, they do not determine whether an operational decision should actually be executed. This demonstration explores that decision gap through an Operational Intelligence methodology.

Operational Intelligence Methodology

CIIP

If a vendor or input fails, what else breaks with it — concentration of exposure weighted by how fragile that vendor actually is.

OVIS

Do we even know what is happening right now — how long, and how unpredictably, an outcome stays unknown after a record is created.

POIS

Could we have seen this coming — whether an upstream signal genuinely leads downstream defects or delays, not just correlates with them.

ODEX

Acts as the governance layer to determine whether a recommendation should be executed.
